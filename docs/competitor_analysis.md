# RealSingles - Competitor Analysis & Industry Research

**Research Sources:** The League, Hinge, Bumble, Raya

**Purpose:** Industry best practices for premium dating app positioning.

---

## Key Findings

### The League
- **Positioning:** Exclusive, career-focused
- **Verification:** LinkedIn required, 10-20% acceptance rate
- **Unique feature:** 100+ life goals system with filtering
- **Bio:** 190 characters max
- **Education filtering:** "No preference" to "highly selective" tiers

### Hinge
- **Positioning:** "Designed to be deleted" - serious relationships
- **Prompts:** 85+ options, 3 required, 150 chars each
- **Organization:** Vitals / Virtues / Vices categories
- **Media:** 6 photos required, voice/video prompts optional
- **Standout features:** Prompt-based profiles drive better conversations

### Bumble
- **Positioning:** Women-first, safety-focused
- **Interests:** 200+ badges
- **Prompts:** 3 optional, 160 chars each
- **Bio:** 300 characters
- **Filters:** Comprehensive including exercise frequency

### Raya
- **Positioning:** Creative industry, ultra-exclusive
- **Media:** Photo slideshow with music
- **Verification:** Instagram required, committee review, 8% acceptance rate

## Industry Standards Applied

### Required Fields (All Platforms)
Name, Age, Gender, Location, Height, 1+ Photo, Dating Intentions, 3 Prompts, Occupation, Education, Phone Verification

### Strongly Recommended
Family Plans, Children Status, Religion, Politics, Drinking, Smoking, Marijuana, 5-10 Interest Badges, Bio (250 chars), Marital Status

### Premium Features
Voice Prompts, Video Intro, Life Goals with filtering

---

## Differentiators

1. **Reward System** - Points for sharing profiles, referrals, inviting friends
2. **Event-Centric** - Converts digital matches into real-world connections
3. **Virtual Speed Dating** - Time-slot based video meetings
4. **Verification Tiers** - Basic (selfie), Photo-verified, ID-verified
5. **Review System** - User reviews from people who know them
